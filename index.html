Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know

Still have questions? Email support.

How does the pricing actually work?+

As one of the first 100 founders, you pay $97 one-time today and get a full year of EventPro Suite™ access — every tool currently available and every new tool added during your year at no extra cost.

After your first year, you move to the $29/month founder rate — yours to keep while subscribed. Once the 100 founder spots are gone, pricing for everyone new starts at $49/month.

What happens if I cancel my subscription later?+

The $29/month founder rate is yours to keep while you stay subscribed. If you cancel your subscription, you'll lose the founder rate. Rejoining later would be at the then-current regular price ($49/mo or higher).

This is how we protect the value of the founder rate for members who stay with us.

Is there a refund policy?+

Yes — 14-day money-back guarantee on your $97 founder payment. If EventPro Suite™ isn't what you expected, email support@eventprosuite.com within 14 days and we'll refund you in full. No hoops, no "reason required."

What tools are actually available right now?+

Event Quote Pro™ is live and working right now. You can log in, set up your brand, and start generating professional client quotes in minutes. It supports 80+ event services, 14 currencies, your logo and colors, tax settings, and PDF export.

Contract Builder Pro™, Profit Calculator Pro™, Event Prep Pro™, and Vendor Finder Pro™ are the next tools in development. As each launches, founders get them automatically at no extra cost during your first year.

Who is EventPro Suite™ built for?+

Event planners, party businesses, balloon artists, event decorators, bounce house operators, event rental companies, photographers, DJs, caterers — anyone running a service-based event business who needs to send professional quotes, track numbers, and look legit to clients.

It's especially built for solo operators and small teams who don't have in-house admin or designers.

Does it work outside the United States?+

Yes. EventPro Suite™ supports 14 currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, NGN, GHS, ZAR, and more. Your quotes are generated in the currency you choose, with your country-appropriate tax settings.

Is my data secure?+

Yes. Your account, profile, and quotes are stored in a secure cloud database with industry-standard encryption. Payments are processed by Stripe — we never see or store your card details.

Can I upgrade or change my subscription later?+

Your founder pricing is locked. As long as you stay subscribed continuously, your $29/month founder rate doesn't change — even if regular pricing increases for new customers down the road.

Who runs EventPro Suite™?+

EventPro Suite™ is built by Monica, founder of Bear Hug Events (a real, operating event business in Orlando, FL) and the Queen of Side Hustles brand. Every tool is designed from the inside of a real event business — not from a tech founder guessing what event pros need.

Founders get direct access to Monica and real input into what gets built next.
