How to publish your site (60 seconds)

Once you download the ZIP, here's how to take it live. No coding. No CLI. Just drag and drop.

1
Unzip the file you just downloaded Find your-site.zip in your Downloads folder. Double-click it — it expands into a folder with your index.html + a README. Your logo and photo are already baked in — no extra files to manage.
2
Go to Netlify Drop Open app.netlify.com/drop in a new tab. (Free Netlify account — sign up in 30 seconds if you don't have one.)
3
Drag the unzipped folder onto the page Netlify will upload everything and give you a live URL within seconds. Your site is now public on the internet.
4
Add your custom domain (optional) If you bought your domain (like yourbusiness.com), hook it up in Netlify under Domain Settings. Full instructions are in the README inside your ZIP.
